USAGE

Macro layout (section gaps, page padding, grid gutters) sits on 8px multiples, so blocks line up on an invisible grid. The single 4px step is the one sub-multiple, reserved for tight optical nudges like icon gaps and chip padding. Prose holds to the 42rem measure (var(--measure)) regardless of how wide the surface gets.
