Shape & elevation

Corners stay quiet: 8px on fields, 12px on containers, full pills only on chips and buttons. Height is earned by one soft, warm-tinted shadow, never a hard black drop.

RADII

--r-field · 8px · inputs, fields
--r-card · 12px · containers, cards
--r-pill · 999px · chips, buttons only

Containers never exceed 12px (oversized-radius ban). The pill is reserved for chips and buttons, never a content panel.

ELEVATION · one soft tinted shadow

A panel that floats a little.

One shadow, tinted with a trace of brand green so the lift reads warm against the warm-white page instead of a cold black halo.

box-shadow: 0 18px 40px -22px color-mix(in oklab, var(--accent) 22%, var(--shadow-tint))

The tint mixes a little --accent into --shadow-tint, never pure black. Ghost-card ban: a single element never carries both a visible border and a blurred shadow, so this panel takes the shadow and drops the border.
