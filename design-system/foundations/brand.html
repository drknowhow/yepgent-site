The sprout is the promise drawn small: stem and two leaves, accent fill, growth over time. It is the symbol, never a decoration. The wordmark stays lowercase; the final period carries the one committed green, the same dot that ends a sentence cleanly.

// the committed-green rule

accent ≤ ~12% of any surface everything else, neutral

Green is committed, not sprinkled. It earns its budget by carrying only what matters:

  • the yepgent. wordmark and its dot
  • links, in body and footer
  • the single primary CTA per surface

Past that, the page stays warm-white and quiet. The restraint is the brand: when the green appears, it means something.
