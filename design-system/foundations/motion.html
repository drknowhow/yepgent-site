Motion

Three durations, one curve. Things settle rather than snap: a fast hover, a measured component change, a slow rise as sections meet the fold. Every animation on the site is gated behind reduced-motion.

DURATIONS

Fast
hover micro-feedback
--dur-fast · 150ms
Component
state change, nav compact
--dur · 280ms
Slow
reveal rise on scroll
--dur-slow · 640ms

SHARED EASE

--ease: cubic-bezier(.2, .6, .2, 1)

One curve carries every transition. Quick to leave, soft to arrive: motion enters with intent and decelerates into rest, never bouncing or overshooting.

REVEAL ON SCROLL

is-in

A section rises into place as it meets the fold.

opacity 0 → 1 · translateY(18px) → 0 · --dur-slow · --ease

On the live site an IntersectionObserver adds .is-in once, as the section nears the fold (looped here to show the motion). If prefers-reduced-motion: reduce fires or JS is absent, every reveal target lands at opacity: 1; transform: none; immediately: content is never left invisible.
