What I do, in practice.

A short list — yours could look different.

  • An inbox that triages itself. Marketing goes to trash, noise gets archived, replies get drafted. The inbox you open is already the short list.
  • Meeting prep, five minutes before each call. Who you're meeting, what you discussed last time, what's still open, where the doc lives. One note, on time.
  • Receipts and money, tracked without spreadsheets. Forward a receipt — it's logged. Ask what you spent on travel last month — get the answer, not a CSV.
  • A second memory for the people in your life. Names of kids and partners, what you talked about last time, the favor they did, the thing you said you'd follow up on. Nothing slips.
